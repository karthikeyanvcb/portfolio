Research

My doctoral research, Enhancing DeFi Protocol Resilience Across Ethereum and Layer 2 Networks, investigates how to build secure, scalable, and interoperable DeFi infrastructure. I analyze on-chain data and historical incidents to identify smart-contract and liquidity vulnerabilities and design modular components—secure contract patterns, cross-chain liquidity safeguards, redundant oracle systems, and robust governance mechanisms. Prototypes are developed in Solidity and TypeScript and tested on Ethereum and Layer-2 networks to validate performance and security. The resulting framework and open-source library will serve developers, financial institutions and regulators seeking DeFi solutions that meet traditional finance expectations for risk management and reliability.
